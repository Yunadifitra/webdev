About

Here's our story begins

  • 2020

    The Beginnings

    As a group friend consisted of 4 people, we found ourself looking at the same problem about how boring shoes style these day is. The artist of our group, Feren, volunteered herself to paint into our shoes and the result is amazing!

  • 2021

    Business was born

    After see the opportunity in Feren talent, Yunadi, our CEO, propose to monetised this amazing talent We try to make our proposal so that we could make this idea come true.

  • 2022

    Our First Real Step

    Our proposal was approved, and we got founding from several investor and open up our first studio located in Jakarta.
